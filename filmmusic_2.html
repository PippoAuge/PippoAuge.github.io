Amargo

This project is the undergraduate thesis of Jacob Victoria, a student at USC's School of Cinematic Arts and tells the story of his grandmother and grandson who have lost touch since the grandfather's death.

I composed the score for Amargo, which consists of piano, violin, cello, double bass and my own synths.

In addition to the organc instruments, I added several synthesizers that I adjusted using Omnisphere's granular synthesizer to accentuate the emotional moments, especially when the grandfather is introduced.

The whole short movie can be watched here: https://vimeo.com/801344002
